WWF' History

The World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) is an international oragnization working on issues regarding the consevation, research and restoration of the World Wildlife fund. WWF was founded in 1961.

WWF's Symbol

The Panda has become the symbol of WWF. The well-known panda logo of WWF originated from a panda named Chi Chi that was transferred from the Beijjing Zoo to the London Zoo in the same year steablishment of WWF.
