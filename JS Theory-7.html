Events are used to call the functions

  1. OnClick
    (Function will be called when you click.)
  2. OnMouseOver
    (Function will be called when you mouseover.)
  3. OnDBlClick
    (Function will be called when you double click.)
  4. OnChange
    (Function will be called when you change the content of input box.)
  5. OnLoad
    (Function will be called when you open the webpage.)
  6. OnUnLoad
    (Function will be called when you close the webpage.)
